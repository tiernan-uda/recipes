Ingredients:
 - 15oz can red kidney beans
 - 1 medium sized onion, diced
 - 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
 - 5 cloves garlic, minced
 - 15oz can tomato sauce
 - 6oz tomato paste
 - 2 tablespoons chili powder
 - 1 tablespoon ground oregano
 - 1 teaspoon cumin
 - 1 teaspoon ground basil
 - 2 teaspoon salt
 - 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Directions:
 1. Saute onion and bell pepper until onion is slightly translucent.
 2. Add the tomato sauce, tomato paste, beans, chili powder, oregano, basil,
    garlic, cumin, salt, and cayenne.
 3. Stir, then cover and simmer over low heat for one hour.
 4. Serve with warm bread and butter.
